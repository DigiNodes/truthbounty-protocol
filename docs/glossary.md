### Claim
A statement submitted for verification.

### Verification
A stake-backed assertion of whether a claim is true or false.

### Verifier
A user who stakes tokens to verify claims.

### Reputation
A score representing historical accuracy of a verifier.

### Confidence Score
A probability-like measure representing certainty of a claim outcome.

### Dispute Window
A time period during which verifications can be challenged.

### Slashing
Economic penalty applied to incorrect or malicious verifiers.

### Sybil Resistance
Mechanisms preventing one user from appearing as many.

### Reputation Anchor
On-chain commitment to off-chain reputation state.

### Public Good
Infrastructure that benefits all users and is non-extractive.
